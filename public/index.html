PWA Support

You can install writevoid.com to your dock/desktop and use 100% offline.

How to use

  • Typing starts the fade timer. After 5 seconds of inactivity, text begins to fade.
  • Hardcore mode: text fades out over 5 seconds.
  • Focus mode: text fades out over 10 seconds.
  • Faded-out text is permanently deleted - there is no recovery.
  • Once you exceed the word limit, a download button appears.
  • Download is in plain .txt format.
  • Fade timer is paused while the download dialog is open and until resuming typing
