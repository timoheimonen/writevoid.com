About writevoid

Updated: 2026-04-08
← Back to writevoid.com

writevoid is a minimalist, distraction-free writing app where your words are temporary by design. If you stop typing, text fades and is permanently removed.

The goal is simple: maintain flow by removing the pressure to polish while drafting.

How writevoid works

  • You type in a clean browser editor with no account required.
  • If you pause typing, your text starts to fade.
  • After fading completes, text is deleted with no recovery option.
  • When you pass a word target, you can download your draft as a plain .txt file.

Why writers use it

  • Works as a focused writing sprint tool.
  • Helps reduce over-editing during first drafts.
  • Encourages momentum with built-in urgency.

Privacy and offline support

writevoid does not include ads or third-party analytics. The app supports offline use through PWA caching and stores only local preferences in your browser.

FAQ

Is writevoid free?
Yes, it is free to use.

Do I need to create an account?
No. There is no login or profile system.

Can I use writevoid on mobile?
Yes. It works in modern mobile browsers and can be installed as a PWA.
